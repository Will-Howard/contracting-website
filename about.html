Recent work

BlueDot Impact — contractor, past year

BlueDot runs courses on AI safety and is one of the key pipelines for people transitioning into the field. When I joined, the platform was running on Bubble and reading directly from Airtable as its database — which meant the site was slow, unreliable, and every new feature required working around Airtable's limitations rather than just writing normal database queries. I built a sync service that replicates their data to Postgres, and helped migrate the platform off Bubble onto their own website. It now serves around 10,000 monthly active users.

A few pull requests from the past year:

  • The database sync service — the Airtable-to-Postgres replication I mentioned above
  • Admin user impersonation — staff couldn't see the platform from a user's perspective, which made it slow to debug issues
  • Facilitators view participant responses — course facilitators needed to see what participants had written in exercises

Centre for Effective Altruism — Tech Lead, ~2 years

Tech Lead on the EA Forum (and the shared codebase with LessWrong). Cut cloud costs by about $60k/year by right-sizing our Kubernetes cluster and cleaning up how we used cloud resources. Built out internal data infrastructure, joining up analytics, CRM, and programme data so the growth team had a single view across programmes. Performance work included setting up CDN caching and optimising how pages load — got page loads under 2.4 seconds, down from around 4.
